# Product Requirements Discussion Plan

## Status

_Not started. Update when active._

**Objective:** Capture stakeholder needs and define clear product requirements for a new feature or project area.

## Goals

- Gather input from users, customers, and internal stakeholders.
- Document success criteria and constraints.
- Ensure requirements are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART).

## Participants

- Product manager
- Engineers
- Designers
- QA lead
- (Optional) End-user representatives

## Agenda

1. Introduction and context
2. Review existing problems and user stories
3. Brainstorm potential solutions and scope
4. Define acceptance criteria for each requirement
5. Identify dependencies and constraints
6. Assign owners and next steps

---

## Deliverables

- `requirements.md` summary document stored in `docs/` or similar
- Link to plan: [product-requirements-plan.md](plans/product-requirements-plan.md)
- Approval sign-off from stakeholders
